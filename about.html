The Spirit of Mubuyu

Where Wilderness
Meets Unmatched Comfort

Nestled next to the Kafue River in Zambia, Mubuyu Bush Camp offers guests an unforgettable experience blending luxury and adventure. Our intimate camp features 4 exclusive chalets, each with 3 king-sized beds, plus optional outdoor tenting for up to 20 guests. Enjoy guided wildlife walks with professional guides and witness animals just feet away from your accommodation.
