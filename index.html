About Mubuyu Bush Camp

Mubuyu Bush Camp is located in the heart of Kafue, Zambia, near rivers and wildlife-rich areas. We focus on sustainable, authentic safari and fishing experiences. Activities include guided game drives, walking safaris, bird watching, and fishing. Stay in comfortable bush accommodations designed to blend with nature.

Safari & Fishing Experience

  • Game Drives: Morning and evening drives to see lions, elephants, giraffes, and other wildlife.
  • Walking Safaris: Explore the bush up close with expert guides.
  • Bird Watching & Photography: Capture Zambia’s natural beauty.
  • Fishing Trips: Enjoy peaceful river fishing, perfect for beginners and experienced anglers alike.
