The Destination

A Jewel in Kafue National Park

Welcome to Mubuyu Bush Camp, a premier luxury safari lodge in Kafue National Park, Zambia. Nestled along the banks of the Kafue River, our camp offers an exclusive gateway to one of Africa’s largest and most pristine wilderness areas.

Unlike crowded safari destinations, Kafue National Park remains a hidden gem, offering untouched landscapes and intimate wildlife encounters. From the roar of lions at dawn to the silent glide of crocodiles in the river, every moment at Mubuyu is an immersion into the true African bush.

As a family-owned bush camp, we prioritize authenticity without compromising on comfort. Our location allows for diverse activities, including boat safaris, walking safaris, and game drives, ensuring you experience the full breadth of Zambia’s biodiversity.

Seasonality Guide

Best Time to Visit Kafue National Park

Understanding the seasons in Zambia is key to planning your perfect luxury safari in Kafue National Park. Mubuyu Bush Camp operates during the prime wildlife viewing months, ensuring you get the most out of your stay.

The Green Season (April - May)

The bush is lush and vibrant following the rains. It's a photographer's paradise with clear skies, dramatic sunsets, and newborn wildlife. The river is full, making for excellent boating safaris.

Peak Winter (June - August)

Cooler temperatures and drying bush make for comfortable game drives and walking safaris. Wildlife begins to congregate around the river, offering consistent sightings of elephants and hippos.

Heat of the Season (Sept - Nov)

As the plains dry out, predator action heats up. This is the best time for dramatic wildlife encounters as animals flock to the remaining water sources. Ideal for fishing enthusiasts.
