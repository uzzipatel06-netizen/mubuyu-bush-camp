Getting Here

Your journey to the heart of the Kafue is part of the adventure. We offer several ways to reach our remote riverside retreat.

By Air

The fastest way to reach us is via domestic flight from Lusaka International Airport. We can arrange private charters or scheduled transfers to the nearest airstrip.

By Road

For those who prefer the scenic route, road transfers can be arranged from Lusaka. The drive offers a glimpse into the Zambian countryside before entering the park.
