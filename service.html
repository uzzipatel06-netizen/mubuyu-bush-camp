The Guest Experience

Unfolding Adventures
at Mubuyu Bush Camp

01
Sunrise

Awaken to the Wild

Coffee on your veranda as the Kafue mist clears. Watch as the morning wildlife begins its descent to the riverbank.

02
Morning

Intuitive Exploration

Led by professional guides, embark on a walking safari or game drive tailored to the day's wildlife movements.

03
Midday

The Art of Rest

Return to the retreat of your luxury chalet. A time for quiet reading, photography, or simply observing the river's flow.

04
Evening

Sunset Gathering

Sundowners by the water as the light turns gold. Shared stories around the campfire followed by dining under the stars.
