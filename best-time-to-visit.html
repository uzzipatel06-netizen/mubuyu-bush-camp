Best Time to Visit

Kafue National Park is a seasonal wilderness, with each time of year offering a unique perspective on its diverse ecosystems.

Dry Season (May - October)

This is the classic safari season. As the landscape dries out, wildlife congregates around the river, making for exceptional sightings.

  • Highlights: Peak wildlife viewing, clear skies, pleasant days.
  • Activities: Walking safaris are at their best.

Emerald Season (November - April)

The rains transform the park into a lush, green paradise. This is a time of rebirth and incredible birdlife.

  • Highlights: Stunning landscapes, newborn animals, world-class birding.
  • Note: Some areas may be less accessible due to water levels.
