Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know to prepare for your intimate safari experience in the Kafue.

What should I pack?

Neutral-colored clothing (khaki, olive, beige) is best for safaris. Don't forget a hat, sunscreen, binoculars, and a good camera. Evenings can be cool, so a light jacket is recommended.

Is it safe?

Safety is our top priority. Our guides are highly trained professionals, and the camp is designed to ensure guest security while maintaining an authentic bush feel.

Do I need a visa?

Visa requirements for Zambia vary by nationality. Many visitors can obtain a visa on arrival or apply for an e-visa in advance. We recommend checking with your local embassy.

Are kids allowed?

Due to the intimate and wild nature of the camp, we generally recommend Mubuyu for children aged 12 and over, though we can make exceptions for exclusive camp bookings.
