Sample 3-Day Safari Itinerary

Experience the rhythm of the wild. This sample itinerary offers a glimpse into the intimate and immersive journey that awaits you at Mubuyu.

Day 1: Arrival & Immersion

Arrive at camp in time for a warm Zambian welcome and a riverside lunch. In the afternoon, embark on your first guided game drive as the sun begins to set over the Kafue.

Day 2: The Heart of the Wild

Start with an early morning walking safari to track wildlife on foot. After a relaxing afternoon at camp, enjoy a boat safari or another game drive, followed by dinner under the stars.

Day 3: Final Farewell

A final morning activity—perhaps a short walk or birding session—before a leisurely brunch and your transfer back to the airstrip, taking memories of the Kafue with you.
