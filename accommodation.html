The Lodging

Luxury Riverfront Chalets

Mubuyu Bush Camp features four intimate chalets designed for maximum comfort and an authentic bush experience.

Our four exclusive chalets are designed to provide the ultimate blend of comfort and immersion. Positioned directly on the riverbank, each chalet offers a private front-row seat to the theatre of the African bush.

Constructed from natural stone and thatch, the chalets stay cool during the day and warm at night. Inside, you'll find a haven of relaxation featuring premium bedding, spacious en-suite facilities, and thoughtful touches that make your stay effortless.
