Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Mubuyu Bush Camp ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data we hold about you. This policy outlines how we handle information collected via our website and booking inquiries.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you make an inquiry or booking, including your name, email address, phone number, and any specific preferences or requirements for your stay.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for:

  • Responding to your inquiries and processing bookings.
  • Communicating important arrival information.
  • Improving our services based on guest feedback.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with the site. This data is collected anonymously and helps us improve the user experience. You can opt-out of this by adjusting your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram) and WhatsApp. Please note that these services have their own privacy policies.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mubuyubushcamp@gmail.com.
